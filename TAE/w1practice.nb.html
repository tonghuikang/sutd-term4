Week 1 Practice

Load the dataframe mtcars available with the base R installation in data. The data was extracted from the 1974 Motor Trend US magazine, and comprises - fuel consumption - 10 aspects of automobile design - performance for 32 automobiles (1973-74 models).

library(ggplot2)  
head(mtcars)
  1. Use ggplot() to plot the weight versus miles per gallon and comment on the relationship. Add to this plot, a coloring of the points based on the number of cylinders, and the sizing of the points based on the displacement (volume) of the car.
acol <- ggplot(mtcars,aes(x=wt, y=mpg, color=cyl, cex=disp))
acol + geom_point(na.rm=T) + geom_smooth(na.rm=T)

  1. Use the tapply() function to compute the standard deviation of the mpg for groups with the same number of cylinders

Refer to: https://www.rdocumentation.org/packages/base/versions/3.6.1/topics/tapply
